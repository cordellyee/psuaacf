MISSION

Turning lost Asian American students into life-long Christ-centered laborers.

VISION

To share the love of Jesus Christ with the world, including every Asian-American student at Penn State, by seeking to...

Win

Share the love of Jesus so that students can know Him and receive Him as their Lord and Savior.

Build

Grow in our relationship with God through His Word and through the fellowship, encouragement, and accountability of small groups and friendships.

Send

Equip students to win, build, and send others in order to help fulfill the Great Commandment (Matthew 22:37-39) and the Great Commission (Matthew 28:18-20).

We hope that AACF can be a safe place where you can grow your relationship with God!
